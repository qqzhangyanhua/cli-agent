    pattern: 文件匹配模式 (*, *.py等)
    recursive: 是否递归列出子目录

Returns:

        "success": bool,
        "path": str,
        "files": List[Dict],
        "directories": List[Dict],
        "total_files": int,
        "total_dirs": int,
        "error": str (if failed)
    }
